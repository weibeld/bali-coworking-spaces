## Grounding Lite
[Grounding Lite](https://developers.google.com/maps/ai/grounding-lite) is an experimental service by Google that uses the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** to ground LLM responses in real-world Google Maps data. It is particularly suitable for high-level, natural-language-driven discovery (e.g., "Find coffee shops nearby") and for applications that require a lightweight, interactive mapping interface without the overhead of full structured API integrations.
